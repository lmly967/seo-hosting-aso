The Basics of SEO Hosting

What is it?

SEO Hosting (Multiple IP Hosting) is the art of hosting your SEO marketing efforts across multiple Class C across multiple geographically diverse locations. Search engine optimization involves creating an online marketing campaign that stretches across multiple websites, IPs, domain names, Class Cs, and DNS.

Why use it?

Search engines award a higher ranking to a site that has links pointing on it from related sites located on unique Class C IPs.

What we offer?

We offer our clients custom hosting solutions using dedicated Class C IPs from up to 250+ unique Class C IPs across multiple locations in the United States and Europe.
